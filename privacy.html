We process your data to respond to enquiries, schedule and deliver plumbing services, manage appointments, and keep records (lawful basis: contract). We may also use data for service improvement and basic analytics (lawful basis: legitimate interests).

We share your data only with our employees or subcontractors who need it to deliver the service, and with service providers that help us run communications or website hosting. We do not sell your data.

We keep enquiry and job records only as long as needed to deliver services, manage accounts, and meet legal or accounting obligations. When data is no longer required, we delete or anonymise it.
